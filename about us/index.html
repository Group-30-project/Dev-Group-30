About Abyssinia Jobs

Welcome to Abyssinia Jobs, your premier platform for connecting talent with opportunity.

Established in 2025, we're dedicated to providing the best job matching experience with a focus on accessibility, trust, and innovation.

We recognized that millions of blue-collar and informal workers lack access to online job opportunities because existing platforms are designed for white-collar professionals. Employers in sectors like construction, agriculture, and domestic services struggle to find trustworthy, skilled workers quickly.

This results in lost income for workers and inefficiencies for employers. Abyssinia Jobs was created to solve these problems with an inclusive platform accessible to everyone.

Our Vision & Impact

To become the leading job portal in the world, known for innovative matching technology and exceptional service that empowers both workers and employers.

Statically, we planned to achieve 1 million registered users and 100,000 active job postings within our first 5 years of operation. We aim to create a vibrant community where talent meets opportunity.
